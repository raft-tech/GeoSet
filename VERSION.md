# GeoSet Version

**Current Version:** 6.0.32
**Based on:** Apache Superset 6.0.0

> GeoSet syncs with Superset minor releases and follows [Semantic Versioning](https://semver.org/).

## Versioning Policy

- **MAJOR** (X.0.0) — Tied to the upstream Superset major version
- **MINOR** (0.X.0) — New GeoSet features, enhancements, or non-breaking upstream rebases
- **PATCH** (0.0.X) — Bug fixes and minor improvements
